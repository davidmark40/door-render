Common Garage Door Problems We Fix

1. Broken Springs

Garage door springs are under a lot of tension, and over time, they can wear out or break. This is one of the most common issues we encounter, and it’s also one of the most dangerous to fix on your own. Leave it to our experts to handle it safely and efficiently.

2. Misaligned Tracks

If your garage door isn’t opening or closing smoothly, the tracks might be misaligned. This can happen due to wear and tear or accidental damage. Our technicians will realign the tracks and ensure everything runs like a well-oiled machine.

3. Faulty Openers

A malfunctioning garage door opener can be incredibly frustrating. Whether it’s a remote control issue or a problem with the motor, we’ll get to the root of the problem and fix it in no time.

4. Damaged Panels

Dented or cracked panels not only affect the appearance of your garage door but can also compromise its functionality. We’ll replace or repair the damaged panels, restoring both the look and performance of your door.

Tips for Maintaining Your Garage Door

While we’re always here to help, a little maintenance can go a long way in preventing costly repairs. Here are some simple tips to keep your garage door in great shape:

  • Regular Inspections: Check for signs of wear and tear, such as frayed cables or loose hardware.
  • Lubricate Moving Parts: Keep hinges, rollers, and tracks well-lubricated to ensure smooth operation.
  • Test the Balance: Disconnect the opener and manually open the door halfway. If it doesn’t stay in place, the springs may need adjustment.
  • Keep It Clean: Wipe down the door and tracks regularly to remove dirt and debris.
